### 🛒 Stocked Up

Stocked Up is a smart grocery and pantry management application that I designed and built to help users reduce food waste, stay organized, and make more informed shopping decisions. The platform allows users to track pantry inventory, create shopping lists, discover recipes based on available ingredients, and locate fresh food sources in their local communities. One of the project's key features is support for farmers market shopping, helping users find local markets and incorporate fresh, locally sourced produce into their purchasing decisions. Stocked Up combines technology, sustainability, and everyday convenience to create a more efficient and thoughtful grocery shopping experience.

Try Stocked Up: [Stocked Up](https://cozy-inventory-ai.lovable.app)

### 💧 FreshWater Finder

FreshWater Finder is a web application that I designed and built to help users locate, explore, and access freshwater resources in their area. The platform is focused on improving awareness of water availability while promoting sustainability, environmental stewardship, and community access to essential natural resources. This project combines my interests in technology, environmental sustainability, and problem solving by using digital tools to connect people with valuable freshwater resources and information. 

Try FreshWater Finder: [FreshWater Finder](https://fresh-water-spotter.lovable.app)
